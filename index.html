For Parents

Is Your Child's
Eyesight Getting Worse?

Myopia (short-sightedness) is rising rapidly among Malaysian children. Early detection and proper management can slow down progression significantly. Even a child with clear 6/6 vision may be at high risk if their hyperopic buffer is insufficient.

Our MyopiaCARE programme uses internationally recognised risk assessment frameworks and evidence-based strategies across five pillars:

🔬 Comprehensive Assessment
🌳 Outdoor Time
📏 Working Distance
👓 Optical Intervention
📊 Regular Monitoring
